About SUPER PRIMED

Based in Chennai, India, SUPER PRIMED specializes in exporting high-quality medical devices, instruments, and equipment from Shenzhen, China, to Malaysia’s healthcare sector. We ensure all products meet ISO, CE, and MDA standards, delivering reliable solutions to hospitals, clinics, and distributors.

Our mission is to provide cost-effective, compliant, and timely medical solutions to support Malaysia’s growing healthcare needs.
